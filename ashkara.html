Ashkara

The Volcanic Dominion

Welcome to the Forge of Fire

Ashkara is a land of molten fury and unforgiving heat, where rivers of lava carve through blackened stone and towering volcanoes pierce the crimson sky. Here, the strongest warriors are forged in flame, and those who endure become legends.

The Crimson Expanse

A landscape of molten fury and volcanic destruction where lava flows carve through blackened stone. The Crimson Expanse is where the strongest warriors are tested and proven. Rivers of lava provide natural barriers and sources of power that the Ashkarans have learned to harness and command.

The Underground Kingdoms

Vast subterranean cities carved into living stone house thriving civilizations of incomparable craftsmanship. Here, master smiths forge the greatest weapons and armor in all of Orbis, and the forges never cool.
