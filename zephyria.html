Zephyria

The Desert Kingdom

Welcome to the Golden Sands

Zephyria stretches across an endless ocean of golden dunes beneath a boundless sky. Here, the ancient winds whisper secrets, ruins speak of forgotten empires, and a fierce people have mastered trade, combat, and the very winds themselves.

Mirrored Sands

Vast expanses of golden dunes stretch endlessly beneath the sun. The Mirrored Sands are home to countless nomadic tribes who have perfected the art of desert survival. Ancient ruins dot the landscape, remnants of civilizations lost to time, waiting for those brave enough to seek their treasures.

Sapphire Coast

Where the endless desert meets crystalline waters, the Sapphire Coast is a beacon of trade and prosperity. Bustling ports teem with merchants from across Orbis, while hidden coves shelter secrets of untold wealth. This is where the greatest fortunes are made and legends are born.
