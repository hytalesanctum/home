The Verdant Territories

Empyrea encompasses vast emerald forests, rolling meadows, crystal rivers, and ancient groves protected by the oldest trees in Orbis. Hidden villages and sacred clearings dot the landscape, each with its own unique character and mysteries to uncover.

The Old Grove

An ancient forest where trees stand as tall as mountains. Home to the Council of Elders and keeper of pre-cataclysm knowledge.

Silverflow Rivers

Crystal-clear waters that cut through lush valleys. Essential for irrigation, fishing, and the mystical power they contain.

Verdant Fields

Endless farmlands that feed the nation. Bountiful gardens produce food, herbs, and rare ingredients for alchemy and magic.

Moonlit Glades

Sacred clearings where moonlight touches untouched forest. Home to rare magical creatures and unknown wonders.
