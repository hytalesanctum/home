Pagorea

The Frozen Realm

Welcome to the Land of Eternal Frost

Pagorea is a land locked in eternal winter, where snow falls endlessly and mystical ice crystals glimmer beneath aurora-lit skies. Here, ancient shamans have unlocked the secrets of frost magic, spore alchemy, and communion with the spirits of the frozen void.

The Shattered Peaks

Towering mountains of pure ice pierce the frozen sky, their slopes treacherous and unforgiving. Beneath the glaciers lie ancient secrets buried for millennia. Only the most skilled shamans can navigate these peaks and commune with the spirits that dwell within the eternal ice.

Aurora Groves

Mystical forests of bioluminescent spores and crystalline trees create otherworldly landscapes beneath dancing aurora lights. These sacred groves are where shamans gather to cultivate their powerful spore magic and commune with ancient spirits. The very air shimmers with magic.
