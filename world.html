Welcome to Orbis

Orbis is an ancient world of breathtaking landscapes, mystical energies, and four distinct civilizations. Each culture has developed its own traditions, magic, architecture, and way of life. The realm is vast and varied, from lush forests to scorching deserts, from frozen tundras to volcanic wastelands.

Long ago, a great cataclysm scattered humanity across Orbis, and four mighty civilizations rose to prominence, each claiming dominion over their unique corners of the world.

The Civilization System

Upon joining Sanctum, every player must choose a civilization to call home. This decision shapes your entire journey, determines your allies and enemies, grants unique abilities, and opens doors to exclusive content.

One Choice, Eternal Bond

Your civilization choice is permanent for the season. Choose wisely, for you will share your destiny with your brothers and sisters in arms.
